# Review brief — Lakeshore Health System (fictional)

**Commissioned:** 1 July 2026 · **Due:** ahead of September board cycle

## Question

Does remote patient monitoring (RPM) of heart-failure patients after hospital discharge
reduce readmissions? Secondary outcomes of interest: mortality, patient adherence, cost.

## Scope & criteria

**Include** studies that:
- concern heart-failure patients (any age, post-discharge or chronic management);
- evaluate remote monitoring (telemonitoring, home devices, structured telephone support
  with device data, wearables used for HF management);
- report at least one outcome of interest (readmission, mortality, adherence, cost);
- are primary research or systematic evidence (trial, cohort, pragmatic study, SR/MA).

**Exclude** studies that:
- concern a different patient population (e.g. atrial fibrillation, COPD);
- report none of the outcomes of interest;
- are editorials, opinion pieces, or protocols without results.

## Ground rules (per client)

- Every inclusion/exclusion decision recorded, with the criterion it was decided on.
- Nothing in the report may cite a source that was not screened **in**.
- Excluded sources listed in an appendix, with reasons.
