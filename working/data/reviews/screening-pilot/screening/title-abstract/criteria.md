# Screening criteria — Screening pipeline pilot (mesothelioma criteria)

**Stage:** title-abstract

**Review question.** In mice, how do genetic mutations (germline or engineered) affect susceptibility to malignant mesothelioma following asbestos exposure?

## Inclusion criteria (ALL must plausibly hold to include)
1. In-vivo mouse study (not solely in-vitro; not rat/human as the primary model)
2. A defined genetic modification whose effect on susceptibility is studied
3. Asbestos or mineral-fibre exposure is administered
4. Malignant mesothelioma is a measured outcome (incidence, latency, or burden)

## Exclusion reasons (controlled vocabulary — use exact strings)
- `not-mouse`
- `no-genetic-modification`
- `no-asbestos-exposure`
- `no-mesothelioma-outcome`
- `in-vitro-only`
- `review-or-commentary`
- `not-primary-research`
- `not-susceptibility-focus`
- `wrong-language`
- `duplicate`

Apply the conservative rule: if a record cannot be confidently ruled out, **include** it with `low` confidence.
